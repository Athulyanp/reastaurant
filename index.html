ABOUT

Manthi Aroma always brought the real flavor of authentic Arabian food to the taste buds of Kerala. We kept our golden feathers over 10 locations in Kerala. Also, 2 overseas locations, Qatar and Bahrain are flavored by our real taste. Our experienced chefs prepare each dish with perfect care so that you can experience the real Arabian flavor. We follow the traditional way of cooking Kuzhimandi in a pit underground which brings the Authentic touch. The rice and meat placed on top of it are slow-cooked for 2-3 hours underground. So that our rice is well flavored with the fat gravy dropped from the meat. We offer the perfect dining experience for you and your family. You can experience the original blend of Arabian taste here.
